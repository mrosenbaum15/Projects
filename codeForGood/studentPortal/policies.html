My commitment and responsibilities with PODER

Welcome to PODER. You have selected an educational center for adults who have committed to provide quality education to meet your needs. To help you meet your academic goals, a structure PODER was implemented. The following are the responsibilities of every PODER student:
I agree with the Following:
  • I will arrive on time and will be prepared for every class
  • I will only use my cellphone for emergencies
  • I will come to class with the appropriate attire/dress code (shorts, t-shirts, sombreros/hats, and bandanas are not permissible
  • The classroom is a place of learning, I accept and acknowledge that in some occasions I can bring food to share with classmates
  • I will ask and notify admin at PODER to bring visitors
  • I will use this opportunity to learn English reach my goals
  • I will recommend my knowledge on the organization of PODER to recommend it to others, so they can have these opportunities
Policies of Absences and Tardies:
  • Bring an explanation/note for every absence (medical notes, school notes with date and time)
  • Your communication is key for us. We need you to call us when you cannot attend a class for any reason
Administrative Norms
  • I authorize PODER to take/use photos of students in the classroom or every event that I participate in promotional material until 4 years after this agreement
